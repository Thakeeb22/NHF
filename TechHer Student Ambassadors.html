TechHer 2.0 Student Ambassadors

Intro: The TechHer Student Ambassadors are the living proof that courage, curiosity, and determination can change lives. These girls took the bold step of entering the tech space and have now become leaders, mentors, and role models in their communities. They not only mastered technical skills but also learned leadership, public speaking, mentorship, and advocacy. Maryam Abubakar: Maryam began quietly, observing and learning. Over time, she grew into a confident, creative leader, inspiring peers with her calm determination and innovative thinking. Khadija Qasim: Khadija’s energy and fearlessness made her a natural motivator. She encouraged other girls to step out of their comfort zones, explore new technologies, and believe in their potential. Salamatu Ahmed: Salamatu’s focus, patience, and empathy made her a dependable leader. She guides peers with insight, showing that leadership is about both innovation and care.

Collective Impact

Together, these ambassadors have turned the TechHer program into a movement.

Highlight Quote

“They dared, they learned, they led—and in doing so, they lit the path for thousands to follow.”
