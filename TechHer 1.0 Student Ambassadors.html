TechHer 1.0 Student Ambassadors

Intro: The first cohort of TechHer ambassadors were true pioneers. They embraced technology when opportunities were limited and uncertainty was high. Through courage and determination, they mastered new skills, created projects, and began mentoring their peers. These girls have now become advocates for female participation in technology, inspiring the next generation to explore digital opportunities and leadership roles. Their collective journey demonstrates that leadership is earned through action and courage. They prove that one bold step can ignite a movement, and their stories continue to inspire new participants to aim higher, work harder, and believe in themselves.

Highlight Quote

“The first leap is the hardest, but it creates the courage for every step that follows.”
