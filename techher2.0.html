Breaking Barriers. Building Future Women Leaders in Technology.

TechHer 2.0

Intro: TechHer 2.0 builds upon the foundation laid by the first edition, taking young girls beyond basic digital literacy into advanced technology, creativity, and leadership. Participants engage in coding, web and app development, UI/UX design, cybersecurity, and project-based learning designed to solve real-world problems. The program integrates critical thinking exercises, collaborative projects, and leadership development sessions, creating an environment where girls are not only learners but innovators and problem solvers. Girls are encouraged to take initiative, design projects that impact their communities, and explore entrepreneurial opportunities in technology. TechHer 2.0 teaches participants resilience, determination, and the power of collaboration. By the end of the program, participants leave with more than just skills—they leave with a belief in their potential, the courage to explore new possibilities, and the ability to inspire others in the tech space.
