Medical Outreach — HIV, Malaria & TB Screening

Intro: This initiative celebrates girls, empowers them with knowledge and leadership skills, and encourages them to break societal barriers. Through mentorship, workshops, and direct engagement with female role models, girls gain confidence, learn about rights, and discover how to lead in their communities. The project instills courage, creativity, and ambition. Participants leave with the belief that they are capable of achieving extraordinary things, that they are not limited by societal expectations, and that their contributions are vital for societal progress.
