About Us

Nature’s Heart Foundation Action for Development and Empowerment Initiatives (NHF-ADEI) is a youth-led, women-focused non-governmental organization dedicated to promoting peace, inclusive development, digital empowerment, and community well-being.

We believe that sustainable change begins when communities — especially women and young people — are equipped with the tools, knowledge, and opportunities to drive transformation.

Through advocacy, innovation, and strategic partnerships, we address pressing social challenges across health, education, governance, climate action, and digital inclusion.

Our Mission

To promote peace, gender equality, innovation, and community development by empowering women and youth through technology, education, health awareness, and advocacy for social justice.

Our Vision

A peaceful, healthy, and inclusive society where women, youth, and adolescents thrive in dignity and innovation, contributing meaningfully to sustainable development.

Our Thematics

Promoting and Advocating for Women, Peace, and Well-being

Strengthening women’s participation in peacebuilding, community leadership, and social well-being.

Accessible, Good, and Quality Education and Technology through STEM

Promoting education and digital inclusion for girls through STEM-focused training and innovation programs.

Climate Action

Advocating for environmental protection, climate education, and youth-led sustainable initiatives.

Promotion of Good and Digital Governance through Technology

Using technology to enhance transparency, civic participation, and digital governance.

Youth and Women Participation in Public Processes

Empowering youth and women to take active roles in governance, civic education, and decision-making.

Adolescent Health and Social Well-being

Promoting adolescent health, menstrual hygiene, HIV awareness, and safe motherhood through education and outreach.
