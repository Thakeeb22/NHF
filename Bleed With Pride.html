Bleed With Pride — Menstrual Health and Hygiene Project

Intro: Bleed With Pride empowers girls with knowledge about menstrual health, hygiene, nutrition, and body confidence. Beyond education, the program provides sanitary products and fosters supportive conversations in schools and communities. It challenges harmful myths, reduces absenteeism, and restores confidence in adolescent girls. Girls learn to embrace their bodies, advocate for themselves, and navigate social and academic spaces without fear or shame.
