Mental Resilience and Awareness Project for Adolescents and Young Persons

Intro: Mental health is essential for personal growth, academic success, and social development. This project equips young people with the skills to manage stress, build self-esteem, and navigate life challenges with confidence. Through peer support, professional guidance, and practical exercises, adolescents learn coping strategies and emotional resilience. By addressing stigma and promoting open conversation, the initiative ensures that mental health is valued and understood. Participants develop the tools to lead, support their peers, and thrive in challenging environments.
